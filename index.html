FAQ

Common questions

What industries do you specialise in?

We specialise exclusively in hospitality and lifestyle marketing — restaurants, hotels, bars, and food and drink brands. This focus means we understand what drives bookings, builds loyalty, and creates genuine brand love in these industries.

What services do you offer?

We offer content creation (photography, videography, design), social media management, targeted advertising (Meta & Google), SEO and GEO (including AI search optimisation), web design and development, influencer marketing, and email campaigns.

How do you measure and report on results?

We provide monthly reporting with honest analysis. We track the metrics that matter — bookings, revenue, engagement, follower growth, and ad ROI — and tell you what's working, what's not, and what we're changing. No vanity metrics.

What is GEO and AI search optimisation?

GEO (Generative Engine Optimisation) ensures your brand appears in AI-generated answers from platforms like ChatGPT, Google SGE, and Perplexity. As customers increasingly ask AI where to eat or stay, we make sure your brand is part of the response.

Do you work with businesses outside of London?

Yes. While many of our clients are in London, we work with hospitality brands across the UK and internationally. Our approach is tailored to each business regardless of location.

How quickly can you start delivering results?

Most clients see measurable improvements within the first three months. We've delivered a 58% increase in F&B revenue within three months and a 95% increase in set menu bookings in the same timeframe. Long-term partnerships deliver the strongest results.
